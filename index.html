About

I am a proud graduate with a degree in mechanical engineering. My path towards becoming an engineer with a passion for coding took a significant turn during the 2022 ASUU strike, which allowed me the opportunity to explore new interests and skills. However, my connection with technology and my curiosity about how things work and how to fix them date back to my early years. Since then, I've been on an exciting and ever-evolving journey, continuously learning and expanding my coding skills. Whether it's developing software, designing solutions for real-world problems, or exploring the latest in technology trends, my passion for both mechanical engineering and coding has become an integral part of who I am. I look forward to the endless opportunities and challenges this path will bring and am excited to contribute to the world of technology and engineering in meaningful ways.

From a young age, I found myself captivated by the intricate world of technology. I often disassembled gadgets and devices to understand their inner workings, unraveling the mysteries behind their functionality. This process of dismantling and reassembling not only satisfied my inquisitiveness but also ignited my passion for solving complex problems and mastering the art of making things work seamlessly.

As I embarked on my journey into the realm of coding during that pivotal ASUU strike year, I discovered an entirely new dimension of technology. This world of coding introduced me to the endless possibilities of creating software, automating processes, and building innovative solutions. It was as if I had found the missing piece of the puzzle that allowed me to combine my mechanical engineering knowledge with the power of programming.
